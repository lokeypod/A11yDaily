# A11yDaily Vision

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Living Document
**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Our Vision

A11yDaily exists to become the trusted intelligence platform for the global digital accessibility community.

We envision a future where accessibility professionals no longer spend hours searching dozens of websites, blogs, standards organizations, government agencies, social platforms, and vendor announcements to stay informed.

Instead, they begin each day with a clear understanding of what changed, why it matters, and what action—if any—they should take.

A11yDaily is not simply an aggregator of information. It is a platform that transforms scattered information into connected knowledge.

---

# The Problem We Are Solving

Accessibility information is everywhere.

Important updates are published by standards organizations, government agencies, technology companies, accessibility vendors, researchers, advocates, and community members.

The information is valuable, but it is fragmented.

Professionals spend significant time searching, filtering, validating, and connecting information before they can apply it to their work.

Our goal is to reduce that effort.

---

# What We Aspire to Build

We aspire to build a platform that helps professionals:

- Discover important accessibility developments.
- Understand why those developments matter.
- Connect related information across multiple sources.
- Learn from trusted experts.
- Make informed decisions more quickly.
- Spend less time searching and more time improving accessibility.

---

# Who We Serve

A11yDaily is built for people whose work depends on staying informed.

This includes:

- Accessibility specialists
- Accessibility engineers
- Document accessibility professionals
- UX designers
- Front-end developers
- Product managers
- Accessibility program managers
- Higher education accessibility teams
- Government accessibility professionals
- Consultants and trainers
- Researchers and students

As the platform grows, we will support personalized experiences tailored to different professional roles while maintaining a single, trusted intelligence engine.

---

# What Makes A11yDaily Different

We believe accessibility professionals deserve more than another news feed.

Our platform is built around intelligence rather than aggregation.

Every piece of information should help answer questions such as:

- What happened?
- Why does it matter?
- Who is affected?
- What should I do next?
- What else is related?

Context is as important as content.

---

# Our Long-Term Vision

Over time, A11yDaily will evolve into a platform that connects knowledge across the accessibility ecosystem.

Information from standards, legal developments, document accessibility, assistive technology, AI, research, community discussions, tools, conferences, and education will be connected into a searchable knowledge network.

The platform will support multiple ways of consuming that intelligence, including:

- Web
- Mobile
- Email briefings
- APIs
- AI assistants
- Collaboration tools
- Future delivery channels

The intelligence remains constant. The experience adapts to the user's needs.

---

# What We Will Not Become

We are not building a click-driven news website.

We are not replacing original authors.

We are not generating content for the sake of volume.

We are not allowing AI to obscure or replace authoritative sources.

Our success will be measured by the trust we earn, not the number of articles we publish.

---

# Success Looks Like...

One day, an accessibility professional will say:

> "Before I start my work each morning, I check A11yDaily."

Not because they have to.

Because they know they can trust what they find.
