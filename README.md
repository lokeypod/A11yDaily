# A11yDaily

Accessibility Intelligence for the Modern Web.

A11yDaily is an AI-powered intelligence platform for tracking digital accessibility news, standards, legal updates, tools, community discussions, and emerging trends.

Know what changed. Understand why it matters. Improve what comes next.

Accessibility professionals are expected to monitor standards, legal developments, government guidance, vendor announcements, document accessibility, assistive technology, AI, community discussions, and research.

The information exists.

It is simply scattered across hundreds of websites.

A11yDaily exists to bring that information together, organize it into meaningful intelligence, and help professionals spend less time searching and more time improving accessibility.
